# Privacy Policy

## Last Updated: January 19, 2026

### Introduction
SigLife ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

### Information We Collect

#### Wallet Information
- **Solana Wallet Address**: Used for NFT minting and Solana transactions
- **Transaction History**: Public blockchain data related to your wallet activity
- **Balance Information**: SOL and token balances for gameplay features

#### Game Data
- **Game Progress**: Stored locally on your device (stats, achievements, history)
- **NFT Ownership**: Public blockchain records of minted NFTs
- **Game Preferences**: Stored locally for personalized experience

#### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version for compatibility
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app (no personal identification)

### How We Use Your Information

#### Core Functionality
- Process NFT minting transactions on the Solana blockchain
- Store game progress locally on your device
- Provide wallet connectivity through Solana Seeker

#### Service Improvement
- Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance gameplay
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Optimize performance and user experience

### Information Sharing and Disclosure

#### We Do Not Sell Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy.

#### Third-Party Services
- **Solana Blockchain**: Public ledger where transactions are recorded
- **Metaplex Protocol**: Used for NFT minting (public metadata)
- **Expo Services**: App distribution and crash reporting (anonymous)

#### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

### Data Storage and Security

#### Local Storage
- Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device
- You can delete this data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

#### Blockchain Storage
- NFT ownership and transaction data are permanently recorded on the Solana blockchain
- This data cannot be deleted or modified once recorded

#### Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption for wallet communications
- Local data encryption on device storage
- Regular security audits and updates

### Your Rights and Choices

#### Data Access
- View your wallet address and transaction history through blockchain explorers
- Access locally stored game data through device settings

#### Data Deletion
- Delete local game data by clearing app data or uninstalling
- Blockchain data is permanent and cannot be deleted

#### Opt-Out Options
- Disable analytics through device settings
- Disconnect wallet to stop transaction-related data collection

### Children's Privacy
SigLife is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through in-app notifications or app store updates.

### Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support channels or repository issues.

### Compliance
This application complies with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations where applicable.
